The Positives and Negatives

Positives

  1. Scores in Math, Science, Writing, English improved significantly
  2. More writing and editing because computers make it easier
  3. Eliminates handwriting grading bias
  4. Need digital research skills for college
  5. Facilitate content creation
  6. Communication and information access
  7. Increase technological literacy
  8. Data collection on students: potential for development

Negatives

  1. School owned laptops and student privacy policies
  2. Potential benefits of privacy-risky tech/devices/software
  3. Data collection on students: potential for control
  4. More devices = more risk
  5. Less expertise = more risk
  6. More devices = more data
  7. Data is sensitive. Childrens future at risk
  8. Students with school devices are not safe from privacy issues while BYOD kids are

The Point

We need to adapt to the changing world and business environment. There appears to be positive effects associated with large scale integration of digital technology in classrooms provided that it is done properly, and keeping in mind the many potential negatives. These negatives are either avoidable with enough planning, or must be accepted in order to reap the benefits.
