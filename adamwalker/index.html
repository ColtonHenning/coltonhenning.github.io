Introduction

In the last decade, we have seen the development of technology take over our everyday lives. Today, many people receive almost all of their information on social media, and this has greatly deepened the political divides in our country. The purpose of this website and our acccounts is to give teens and students information that makes them smarter online users when it comes to receiving political information on their social media feed.
